## Style Prompt

JobRaker launches as a blunt, kinetic stomp-typography promo: oversized type hits on a dark operational canvas, acid-green brand accents cut through dense UI/data fragments, and every scene feels like a job-search bottleneck being smashed into a cleaner automated pipeline.

## Colors

- `#050805` Background: near-black with a slight green cast.
- `#F2FFF0` Foreground: bright, high-contrast text.
- `#1DFF00` Brand accent: JobRaker acid green from the app theme.
- `#7CFF72` Secondary glow: softer green for depth and highlights.
- `#FFB020` Impact accent: sparingly used for warning, launch, and beat-hit moments.
- `#172217` Surface: dark panels and UI mock surfaces.

## Typography

- Display: `Anton`, uppercase, heavy, compact, used for stomp hits and launch statements.
- System/data voice: `IBM Plex Mono`, used for labels, terminal readouts, counters, and product UI fragments.
- Brand/body voice: `Questrial`, used for short support lines where the app identity needs to feel familiar.

## Motion

- Primary motion is percussive: scale punches, short horizontal smears, compression, and quick rebounds.
- Transitions use full-frame block wipes and white/green impact flashes, timed like beat hits.
- Product details enter as staggered operational fragments, then resolve into one clear message.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not soften the piece into a calm SaaS explainer.
- Do not introduce purple-blue gradients or generic AI-glow styling.
- Do not use long paragraphs; every line must be readable within two seconds.
- Do not use jump cuts between scenes; every scene change needs a visible impact transition.
