# Jobraker HyperFrames Launch Design

## Style Prompt

Jobraker is presented as a pitch-black agentic command center for job seekers: hostile ATS gravity, glassy terminal/chat UI, explainable proof graphs, and a neon-green counter-agent that turns job hunting from manual form-filling into deployable agent work. The piece should feel premium, kinetic, founder-led, and slightly unhinged without losing product clarity.

## Colors

- `#050706` Core background: near-black with a green cast.
- `#070A08` Secondary background: deeper console surfaces.
- `#0C1510` Panel background: glass command-center cards.
- `#00FF85` Primary accent: Jobraker neon agent green.
- `#00E676` Secondary accent: stable success green.
- `rgba(0,255,133,0.30)` Glow: agent trails, UI scans, state changes.
- `#F5FFF8` Main text: high-contrast foreground.
- `#8A938D` Muted text: metadata, labels, secondary explanation.
- `#FF4D4D` Warning accent: rejection/error states only.

## Typography

- Display voice: `Anton`, uppercase, heavy, compressed, used for hooks and taglines.
- Product/data voice: `IBM Plex Mono`, used for commands, proof labels, counters, and terminal output.
- Human/body voice: `IBM Plex Sans`, used for short explanatory copy and UI labels.

## Motion

- Primary transitions are green-black skew wipes with brief exposure flashes.
- Scene motion is beat-led: rejection cards slam, command palettes snap, proof edges draw, plugin cards expand, and Agent 50 traces a path through work.
- Ambient motion stays finite and deterministic: slow grid drift, glow breathing, and panel scan lines.
- Final second holds steady for screenshot readability.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use purple-blue generic AI gradients.
- Do not turn this into a calm SaaS walkthrough.
- Do not use real company logos in the mock company cards.
- Do not rely on static slides; every scene needs motion, hierarchy, and a metaphor.
- Do not reference missing audio, video, or image files.
